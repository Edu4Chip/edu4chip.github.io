How to join the the program as a student

The program is implemented in different ways at the partner univerisities. Each university integrates Edu4Chip within its existing curriculum, customizing the program according to local regulations and academic structures. You can see how Edu4Chip is implemented across the universities at the Program and curriculum page.

To join the program you need to contact the Edu4Chip referece person at your university. Please find below the contact below.

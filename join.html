Edu4Chip - Joint Education for Advanced Chip Design in Europe

Join the program

The Edu4Chip program is implemented locally by each partner university. While the program shares a common vision and structure, admission and enrollment procedures are handled independently by each institution.

To join as a student, you must apply directly to the relevant degree program at the university of your choice. Below, you can find specific information about each university’s admission procedures (as a link to their program page). You can also see how Edu4Chip is implemented in more detail across the universities at the Program and curriculum page.

Technical University of Munich (TUM)

A new Master's program, Microelectronics and Chip Design, is being developed at TUM with two specialization tracks: Analog/Mixed-Signal and Digital Design. The program includes the full Edu4Chip chip design tape-out experience across four semesters.

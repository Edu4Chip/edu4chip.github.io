What is Tiny Tapeout?

Tiny Tapeout makes chip design accessible to everyone, from complete beginners to seasoned professionals. This initiative simplifies and democratizes the process of creating and manufacturing your very own digital integrated circuits.

Tiny Tapeout is a revolutionary platform that brings your ideas to life by enabling you to design chips that are actually fabricated. It transforms concepts into real silicon, making chip design accessible to everyone, regardless of experience. Best of all, it is affordable!

Workshop Highlights

Matt Venn, the creator of Tiny Tapeout and an experienced electronic engineer specializing in open-source silicon, will be lecturing at the workshop.

The workshop will guide you through the entire process of creating your own chip design:

  • Draw Your First MOSFET: Start with the basics using SiliWiz.
  • Simulate Logic Gates: Design and test digital circuits using Wokwi.
  • Generate a GDS File: Create the layout data for chip manufacturing.
  • Submit Your Design: Submit your custom design to Tiny Tapeout for fabrication.

Who Can Attend?

This workshop is open to everyone, including:

  • High school students
  • University students
  • Academics
  • Professionals from all industries
  • Makers

No prior hardware design experience is required! Just bring your curiosity and creativity.

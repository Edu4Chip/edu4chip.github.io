Edu4Chip - Joint Education for Advanced Chip Design in Europe

Overview

Welcome to the Edu4Chip Summer School 2026, taking place in the beautiful South of France, between Aix-en-Provence and Marseille. The summer school will explore topics in chip design and related areas, with the main theme to be announced soon.

The summer school is designed for Bachelor students and Master students with a background in electrical engineering, computer science and engineering, or physics, the summer school emphasizes practical learning activities.
